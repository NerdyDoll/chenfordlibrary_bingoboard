Chenford Writing Bingo

Click a square to mark it off. Use “Shuffle Board” if you want a fresh random bingo card.

Controls

Shuffle: creates a brand new bingo board.
Reset: clears all current marks from this board.
Download: opens the print view so you can save as PDF.
Lock: disables Shuffle and Reset, but you can still mark squares.
Settings: changes board options, filters, and sharing tools.
Help: shows this guide.

Bingo rules

Mark squares as you complete prompts. A bingo is any full row, column, or diagonal. If free spaces are enabled, they appear on the board with your chosen labels, but they are not automatically marked. You can keep marking squares even after getting one bingo.
Settings
Include free spaces
Free spaces are placed randomly on the board and are not auto-marked.
Prompt filter
Uncheck any prompts you never want included when shuffling.
